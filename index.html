Nokuthula Iyeza Lokuchela

Ukuchela your yard is a way of spiritually cleansing your home to keep the energy peaceful, balanced, and positive.

It focuses on both the physical space and the unseen environment around your home. This is often done by sprinkling salt or herbal mixtures around the yard a few times a month. These are believed to help remove heavy or negative energy that may build up over time.

Cleansing your yard is also seen as a form of protection. It is believed to help keep away harmful spiritual influences such as curses, witchcraft (boloi), or entities like tokoloshi, helping create a safer and more peaceful home for everyone living there.
